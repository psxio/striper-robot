How it works

Three steps to perfect lines.

01

Design your lot in the cloud

Open Strype Cloud, enter an address, and draw parking lines on the satellite map — or import a DXF/SVG. The platform generates optimized waypoints automatically.

02

Deploy & press start

Snap in a paint cartridge, place Strype at the lot origin, and upload the mission. The robot handles the rest — fully autonomous.

03

Come back to perfect lines

Every line GPS-straight, every space identical. Your lot layout is saved in the cloud — re-stripe with one click next season.

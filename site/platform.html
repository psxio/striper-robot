Strype Platform — Quick Start

Getting Started

  1. Search for an address or paste coordinates to find your parking lot
  2. Click + New to create a lot, then use tools or templates to design
  3. Use Templates to quickly place parking rows, handicap spaces, crosswalks, and arrows
  4. Export as .waypoints for Mission Planner or .geojson for reference
